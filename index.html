Who we are

We can provide complete guidance for your requirements in Commercial RO plants, Industrial RO plants and Softeners apart from the domestic equipment models.

All our products are manufactured using premium grade raw material and components which are sourced from the reliable and certified vendors overseas and locally.


We have an expertise team for installation and service in Telengana and Andhrapradesh. we have completed 1000+ commercial water treatment plants and 4000+ Domestic RO in both states.
